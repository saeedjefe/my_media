Saadi, the prominent Persian poem, once said, "Accumulate money and pursue knowledge until the last day of your life, for with riches, you win the respect of fools, but with knowledge, you can gain the esteem of the wise." With mankind being a multifaceted creator, setting specific goals has been always an obsession, especially for the intellectuals. 
Amid the expressions of opposing views, each of which places a particular emphasis on one aspect of human being, a populist campaign promotes the notion that economic progress for the accumulation of riches is the most important goal of life.
