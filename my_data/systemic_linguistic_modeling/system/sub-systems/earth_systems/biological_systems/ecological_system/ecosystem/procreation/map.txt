PROCREATION SYSTEM (PS)
├── Sexual Reproduction System
│   ├── Involves two parents
│   ├── Combines male & female gametes
│   ├── High genetic variation
│   ├── Includes courtship and mating
│   └── Found in most animals and flowering plants

└── Asexual Reproduction System
    ├── Involves one parent
    ├── Produces clones
    ├── Fast and energy-efficient
    ├── Common in plants, bacteria, some animals
    └── Less genetic diversity
