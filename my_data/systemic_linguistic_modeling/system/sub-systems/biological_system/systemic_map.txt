│   ├── Dissolution – senescence, death, decomposition
│   └── Distribution – migration, seed dispersal, gene flow
├── Behaviors (Systemic)
│   ├── Adaptation – physiological or behavioral response to environment
│   ├── Resilience – biological recovery after disruption
│   ├── Vulnerability – sensitivity to disease or habitat loss
│   ├── Rigidity – genetic or behavioral inflexibility
│   ├── Emergence – rise of new traits or behaviors
│   ├── Fragmentation – loss of biodiversity or genetic continuity
│   ├── Homogenization – reduction in variation, monoculture
│   ├── Heterogenization – diversification of traits and species
│   ├── Feedback Loops – hormonal cycles, predator-prey dynamics
│   ├── Repository Growth – accumulation of biodiversity, genetic libraries
│   ├── Entropy – disorder through mutation, extinction, decay
│   └── Self-Improvement – evolution of complex systems, neural learning
