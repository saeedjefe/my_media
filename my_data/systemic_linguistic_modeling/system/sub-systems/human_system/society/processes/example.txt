Input = Migration into a country.

Transformation = Education, work integration.

Output = New skilled workers, cultural innovation.

Distribution = Wealth, knowledge, political power.

Feedback = Elections, surveys, social movements.

Adaptation = Policy reforms to improve integration.

Maintenance = Law enforcement, healthcare support.

Growth = New industries, expanded cities.

Emergence = New cultural identities.

Replication = New communities built.

Collapse/Dissolution (if failure) = Civil unrest or brain drain
