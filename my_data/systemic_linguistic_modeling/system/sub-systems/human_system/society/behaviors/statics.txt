Social Statics
├── Cultural Inertia
│    └── Preservation of traditional customs and resistance to new cultural influences
├── Political Rigidity
│    └── Unchanging political structures, leaders, and ideologies
├── Economic Inflexibility
│    └── Dependence on traditional industries with little innovation
├── Educational Conservatism
│    └── Sticking to old curricula, resisting educational reforms
├── Institutional Stagnation
│    └── Bureaucracies resisting modernization and technological updates
├── Social Role Fixation
│    └── Strict, unchanging social roles based on gender, class, or age
├── Technological Resistance
│    └── Reluctance to adopt new technologies or digital systems
└── Religious Orthodoxy
     └── Strict adherence to traditional religious practices without adaptation
