Human Personality System
├── Entities
│   ├── Core Self
│   ├── Temperament (inborn tendencies)
│   ├── Cognitive Traits (thinking style, openness)
│   ├── Emotional Disposition
│   ├── Behavioral Patterns (habits, reactions)
│   └── Self-Concept (identity, self-esteem)
├── Inputs
│   ├── Genetic Predispositions
│   ├── Early Childhood Environment
│   ├── Family Dynamics
│   ├── Cultural and Social Norms
│   ├── Education and Learning
│   ├── Life Experiences (trauma, success, failure)
│   └── Ongoing Social Feedback
├── Outputs
│   ├── Behavioral Tendencies (introversion, assertiveness)
│   ├── Emotional Reactions (anger, empathy, anxiety)
│   ├── Interpersonal Style (cooperative, dominant, avoidant)
│   ├── Cognitive Responses (problem-solving, interpretation)
│   ├── Self-Expression (through speech, dress, art, choices)
│   └── Life Decisions and Goal Setting
├── Dependencies
│   ├── Interpersonal Reinforcement (validation, rejection)
│   ├── Social Identity Roles (gender, status, group affiliation)
│   ├── Environmental Context (stability, threat)
│   └── Internal Needs (autonomy, belonging, competence)
├── Repositories
│   ├── Long-Term Memory and Narratives
│   ├── Internalized Beliefs and Values
│   ├── Coping Mechanisms
│   └── Personality Traits (Big Five, MBTI, Enneagram types)
├── Processes
│   ├── Self-Reflection and Self-Evaluation
│   ├── Identity Formation and Maintenance
│   ├── Habit Formation and Reinforcement
│   ├── Emotion Regulation
│   ├── Adaptation to Environment
│   ├── Conflict Resolution (internal and external)
│   └── Personality Growth or Defense Mechanisms
├── Modifiers
│   ├── Stress and Pressure
│   ├── Major Life Events (loss, success, change)
│   ├── Physical Health and Neurology
│   ├── Sleep, Nutrition, Hormones
│   ├── Relationships and Feedback
│   └── Spiritual or Philosophical Beliefs
├── Boundaries
│   ├── Core Values and Personal Ethics
│   ├── Emotional Regulation Thresholds
│   ├── Personal Space and Privacy Needs
│   └── Limits of Changeability (trait stability)
├── Behaviors
│   ├── Adaptation (evolving under experience)
│   ├── Resilience (emotional and cognitive recovery)
│   ├── Rigidity (resistance to change)
│   ├── Vulnerability (emotional openness)
│   ├── Pluralism (coexistence of multiple identities)
│   ├── Emergence (new traits or behaviors under stress)
│   ├── Suppression (masking traits)
│   └── Repository Growth (wisdom, self-knowledge, insight)
