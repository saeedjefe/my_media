Suppression
├── Political Suppression
│    └── Blocking of grassroots movements, silencing dissent
├── Cultural Suppression
│    └── Censorship or banning of new artistic, musical, or literary expressions
├── Economic Suppression
│    └── Monopoly practices preventing startups or innovations from emerging
├── Technological Suppression
│    └── Restricting or banning access to disruptive technologies
├── Educational Suppression
│    └── Limiting critical thinking, banning new scientific theories or research topics
├── Linguistic Suppression
│    └── Forbidding the use of minority languages, banning slang or new words
├── Media Suppression
│    └── State or corporate control over news, blocking alternative voices
├── Religious Suppression
│    └── Outlawing new religious movements or interpretations
└── Social Innovation Suppression
     └── Discouraging new social norms, lifestyles, or organizational models
