RESEARCH
├── BY PURPOSE
│   ├── Basic (Pure) Research
│   │   └── Aims to expand knowledge without immediate application
│   └── Applied Research
│       └── Aims to solve practical problems
├── BY APPROACH
│   ├── Quantitative Research
│   │   ├── Descriptive
│   │   ├── Correlational
│   │   ├── Experimental
│   │   └── Quasi-experimental
│   └── Qualitative Research
│       ├── Ethnography
│       ├── Case Study
│       ├── Phenomenology
│       ├── Grounded Theory
│       └── Narrative Analysis
├── BY METHOD
│   ├── Experimental
│   ├── Observational
│   ├── Survey
│   ├── Simulation
│   └── Modeling
├── BY DATA TYPE
│   ├── Empirical Research
│   │   ├── Quantitative (numerical data)
│   │   └── Qualitative (non-numerical data)
│   └── Theoretical Research
│       └── Uses models, analysis, logic rather than data collection
├── BY LOGIC
│   ├── Deductive Research
│   │   └── Tests a hypothesis from theory
│   └── Inductive Research
│       └── Builds theory from observations
├── BY TIME DIMENSION
│   ├── Cross-sectional
│   └── Longitudinal
├── BY SETTING
│   ├── Field Research
│   └── Laboratory Research
├── MIXED METHODS
│   ├── Sequential (Qual → Quant or Quant → Qual)
│   └── Concurrent (both simultaneously)
