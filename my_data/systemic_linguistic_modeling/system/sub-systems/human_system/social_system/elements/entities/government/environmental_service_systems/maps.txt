Paper_Recycling_System
├── Collection
│   └── Input: Paper waste (newspapers, cardboard, office paper)
│   └── Method: Curbside collection, drop-off points
├── Sorting
│   └── Input: Mixed paper
│   └── Process: Sorting by type (cardboard, mixed paper, etc.)
├── Shredding
│   └── Input: Sorted paper
│   └── Process: Paper shredded into small pieces
├── De-inking
│   └── Input: Shredded paper
│   └── Process: Removal of ink using water and chemicals
├── Pulping
│   └── Input: De-inked paper
│   └── Process: Turning paper into pulp (water, chemicals)
├── Drying & Forming
│   └── Input: Paper pulp
│   └── Output: New paper sheets
├── Outputs
│   └── New paper products (recycled paper, cardboard)
├── Goals
│   └── Waste reduction, saving trees, energy conservation

Plastic_Recycling_System
├── Collection
│   └── Input: Plastic waste (bottles, containers, packaging)
│   └── Method: Curbside collection, recycling bins
├── Sorting
│   └── Input: Mixed plastics
│   └── Process: Separation by resin type (PET, HDPE, PVC, etc.)
├── Shredding
│   └── Input: Sorted plastics
│   └── Process: Plastic shredded into small pieces
├── Cleaning
│   └── Input: Shredded plastics
│   └── Process: Removal of contaminants (labels, food residue)
├── Melting & Molding
│   └── Input: Cleaned plastic pieces
│   └── Output: Pellets, new plastic products
├── Outputs
│   └── Recycled plastic items (bottles, new containers)
├── Goals
│   └── Reduce plastic waste, save energy, promote recycling



Metal_Recycling_System
├── Collection
│   └── Input: Metal waste (aluminum cans, scrap metal, industrial metals)
│   └── Method: Curbside collection, drop-off points, industrial pickups
├── Sorting
│   └── Input: Mixed metals
│   └── Process: Separation of ferrous and non-ferrous metals
├── Shredding & Crushing
│   └── Input: Sorted metals
│   └── Process: Shredding or crushing into smaller pieces
├── Smelting
│   └── Input: Shredded or crushed metals
│   └── Process: Melting metals to remove impurities
├── Casting
│   └── Input: Molten metal
│   └── Output: New metal products
├── Outputs
│   └── Recycled metal products (aluminum cans, construction materials)
├── Goals
│   └── Recover valuable metals, reduce mining, and energy consumption

Glass_Recycling_System
├── Collection
│   └── Input: Glass waste (bottles, jars, containers)
│   └── Method: Curbside collection, drop-off points
├── Sorting
│   └── Input: Mixed glass
│   └── Process: Sorting by color (clear, green, brown)
├── Cleaning
│   └── Input: Sorted glass
│   └── Process: Removal of contaminants (labels, residue)
├── Crushing
│   └── Input: Cleaned glass
│   └── Process: Crushing into small pieces (cullet)
├── Melting & Molding
│   └── Input: Cullet
│   └── Output: New glass products (containers, insulation)
├── Outputs
│   └── Recycled glass items (bottles, tiles)
├── Goals
│   └── Reduce landfill waste, conserve raw materials, save energy
