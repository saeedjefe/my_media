PERSONAL SITUATION SYSTEM (PSS)
├── Entities
│   ├── Individual
│   ├── Time
│   ├── Resources
│   ├── Beliefs
│   ├── Life stage
│   └── Social environment

├── Inputs
│   ├── Life events (job loss, relocation)
│   ├── Economic conditions
│   ├── Psychological state
│   ├── Cultural expectations
│   ├── Technology access
│   └── Health condition

├── Outputs
│   ├── Behavioral response
│   ├── Emotional reaction
│   ├── Decision-making
│   ├── Identity shift
│   ├── Feedback for future actions
│   ├── Adaptation or resignation
│   └── Stress or personal growth

├── Dependencies
│   ├── Resource availability
│   ├── Mental and physical health
│   ├── Support networks
│   ├── Past experiences
│   └── Cultural values

├── Boundaries
│   ├── Temporary vs. permanent
│   ├── Self-induced vs. external
│   ├── Private vs. public impact
│   ├── Age-related perspectives
│   └── Severity and acceptance

├── Modifiers
│   ├── Education level
│   ├── Global exposure
│   ├── Technological use
│   ├── Economic conditions
│   ├── Trauma history
│   ├── Health status
│   └── Policy support

├── Repositories
│   ├── Memory of past hardships
│   ├── Cultural scripts and values
│   ├── Digital records (medical, financial)
│   ├── Educational and career archives
│   └── Emotional memory and conditioning

├── Processes
│   ├── Recognizing the situation
│   ├── Evaluating options
│   ├── Responding (acceptance or action)
│   ├── Learning from results
│   ├── Maintaining successful strategies
│   ├── Adapting to new challenges
│   ├── Replicating effective behaviors
│   └── Dissolving ineffective habits

└── Goals
    ├── Emotional balance
    ├── Improvement of conditions
    ├── Long-term security
    ├── Personal development
    ├── Career or life progression
    └── Fulfillment of needs
