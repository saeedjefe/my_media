INCIDENT SYSTEM (IS)
├── Entities
│   ├── Individuals
│   ├── Organizations
│   ├── Systems or Processes
│   └── Timeframe

├── Inputs
│   ├── Human Action
│   ├── Environmental Conditions
│   ├── System Vulnerability
│   └── Past Experience / Memory

├── Outputs
│   ├── Feedback for improvement
│   ├── Policy change
│   ├── Damage or harm
│   ├── Increased awareness
│   └── Behavioral shift

├── Dependencies
│   ├── Level of preparedness
│   ├── Reporting culture
│   ├── Transparency
│   └── Response capability

├── Boundaries
│   ├── Minor vs. Major
│   ├── Intentional vs. Accidental
│   ├── Personal vs. Public
│   ├── One-time vs. Recurring
│   └── Physical vs. Digital

├── Modifiers
│   ├── Training & Awareness
│   ├── Technology use
│   ├── Regulatory environment
│   └── Cultural tolerance for mistakes

├── Repositories
│   ├── Incident reports
│   ├── Historical records
│   ├── Institutional memory
│   └── Digital logs

└── Processes
    ├── Detection → identifying the incident
    ├── Evaluation → understanding its impact
    ├── Response → taking corrective action
    ├── Feedback Loop → learning from the incident
    ├── Maintenance → preventing future problems
    ├── Replication → applying lessons elsewhere
    ├── Dissolution → when similar incidents stop occurring
    └── Adaptation → changing behavior based on past incidents
